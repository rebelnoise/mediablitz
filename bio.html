In a time when so many are claiming "old school this" and "old school that," how many of them can even claim having roots beyond the late 80's? (and quite frankly it shows). Well 'MEDIA BLITZ' claim roots going all the way back to the late 70's, when guitarist 'Tony Fingers' and three friends formed the seminal East Los Angeles punk group 'THEE UNDERTAKERS' - sharing bills with the likes of 'X', 'D.O.A.', 'THE PLUGS', 'THE GEARS', etc.

The rest of the band aren't exactly a bunch of slouches either, as the rhythm duo of bassist 'Rosie Blitz' and drummer 'Serious Mike Lee' would have felt right at home thumping and bashing it out for any number of classic early Boston and New York hardcore groups.

Vocalist 'BEAST' has been best described by onlookers as a combination of Alice from The Bags and Wendy O'Williams from the Plasmatics, dishing out the lyrical portion with authority and tenacity.

MEDIA BLITZ has been going at it since '93, gigging up and down the west coast as well as the midwest - playing shows with 'Naked Aggresssion', D.R.I.', 'Dr. Know', 'Total Chaos', among many others.

While lashing it out live with dead serious intensity, the band also realizes that most people came to have a good time. So while some industry cheezebag that never even liked punk a month ago tries to sell you his latest fake band concoction as "old school" - take it from someone who was really 'there'. MEDIA BLITZ has that special spark of furor and desperation that made the early L.A. punk groups so reviled.

- Brian, founder, Grand Theft Audio Records,
Writer, Flipside and Maximum Rock'N'Roll
